Foods that can harm the thyroid gland

The thyroid gland is one of the most important, but also the most susceptible to organ damage. Its functioning can be disrupted even when eating certain foods. Here are 6 products, that harm the thyroid gland.

1. Sugar.

Limit the use of sweet foods, as if insufficient thyroid function is more likely to trigger diabetes. As we know, sugar is the number one factor for this.

2. Gluten-free.

Studies of doctors have shown that patients with thyroid problems, those who have restricted their gluten intake improve their condition. Therefore, medicine recommends switching to a gluten-free diet if they are present.

3. Soy.

Soy products and all varieties of soy, in particular soy milk, soy sauce, etc., impair the function of the thyroid. According to medical research, soybeans the products stop the absorption of hormones designed to combat thyroid diseases.

4. Cabbage.

Cabbage, as well as other healthy green vegetables such as broccoli, cauliflower, and Brussels sprouts, weaken the thyroid function. Unfortunately, their consumption should be limited for thyroid problems because of the danger of having an excessive amount of iodine in the body. You can only eat ordinary cabbage, if it has undergone heat treatment.

5. Fish.

Fish - especially swordfish, tuna and mackerel contain large amounts of mercury, which is similar in its properties to iodine, so it can cause problems if you suffer from thyroid disease. Excessive mercury consumption increases the risk of developing serious autoimmune problems.

6. Algae.

If you prefer plant-based foods and, in particular, various types of algae, you should know that they contain a lot of iodine. Under certain conditions problems with thyroid are forbidden to eat seafood and products with iodine, as this element can cause complications, especially if you take it in large quantities.

Good for the brain of herbs and spices

Some plants show curative effects on brain activity. They are able to protect brain cells from damage, support brain activity, and improve cognitive abilities, facilitate learning and memorization of new information.

Turmeric.

Turmeric is known for its anti-inflammatory effect and is often used in Asian cuisine. This spice contains curcumin , a powerful antioxidant that prevents the accumulation of free radicals and supports brain activity. In addition, turmeric can have a protective effect on the brain, and it is believed that some of its components protect nerve cells from damage and death.

Lemon sorghum.

Some studies show that lemongrass has a calming effect and is suitable for people who are in a state of people with severe stress, as well as people suffering from anxiety disorders. This plant contains rosemary acid, which improves sleep and is suitable for people suffering from insomnia.

Ginkgo biloba.

Ginkgo biloba has been used for many years to treat people with dementia, and positive results are known effects of this plant on brain activity. It is believed that it increases cognitive function, because it stimulates the brain's blood circulation.

Chamomile.

You have probably heard that chamomile has a calming effect, and tea from this plant is useful at night before going to bed. According to some medical experts for example, chamomile contains a component called luteolin, which reduces inflammatory responses in the brain. This reduces the risk of Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease.

Ginger.

Ginger is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and is also considered natural analgesic. Some studies show that this plant can counteract free radicals and protect brain cells from damage.

Pepper.

One of the most purchased spices in the world helps to absorb some of the beneficial elements from the plants listed above, but it also has useful properties for the brain. One of the main ingredients of black pepper is the alkaloid piperine, which increases cognitive function and relieves signs of depression.
